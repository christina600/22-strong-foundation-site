**Use:** recovery access · high-standard care · return to function · strength, mobility, and support · clear next steps · one-to-one care · no-cost access for eligible participants · mission-aligned access

**Never use:** workshops (Vas explicitly rejects) · healing journey · radical care · disruptive solution · charity care · low-cost wellness · luxury treatment · underserved as a lead descriptor · ecosystem · transformational · self-care oasis

**Tone:** Calm authority with warmth. "Experienced specialist guiding someone through recovery." Not campaign-y, not pity-coded, not influencer-y.
