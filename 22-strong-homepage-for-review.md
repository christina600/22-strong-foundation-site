## About the organization (context for reviewers)

- **22 Strong Foundation** — a 501(c)(3) public benefit corporation (California), EIN 99-1232582, founded 2023.
- **Mission:** provide no-cost access to recovery, strength, and mobility care for **three populations** — veterans, underprivileged youth, and people facing financial barriers.
- **The program — "Better in 90":** a structured 90-day recovery program in three phases, with maintenance care continuing beyond Day 90. Delivered through specialist partner facilities in Orange County / Southern California.
- **Stewardship model:** 100% of donations go to participant care access; 0 paid employees (operates as a donation vehicle).

### Voice & tone rules the copy follows
- **Use:** recovery access, high-standard care, return to function, strength/mobility/support, clear next steps, one-to-one care, no-cost access for eligible participants.
- **Never use:** workshops, healing journey, radical care, disruptive solution, charity care, low-cost wellness, luxury treatment, "underserved" as a lead descriptor, ecosystem, transformational, self-care oasis.
- **Tone:** calm authority with warmth — "an experienced specialist guiding someone through recovery." Not campaign-y, not pity-coded, not influencer-y.
- **Hard rule:** no invented stories, composite participants, AI-generated quotes, or undocumented statistics.

### Visual direction
- **Colors (from the logo):** charcoal `#2D2D2D`, bone cream `#E8DDC8`, burnt orange `#C05010 / #D9601F` (CTA), with secondary camo/earth tones (tan, sage, olive, sandstone, brown).
- **Type:** Inter (body/UI), Fraunces (editorial display serif), JetBrains Mono (labels).
- **Hero:** real photograph of a veteran behind a light wash, dark headline.

### Section 2 — Programs (Better in 90)
- **Eyebrow:** Programs
- **Heading:** Better in 90 — a structured path back to function.
- **Body:** Three phases over 90 days — targeted care, whole-body recovery, and maintenance — with continued support beyond Day 90 for as long as we can provide it.
- **Intro line:** Better in 90 runs in three phases over 90 days, with maintenance care beyond. Eligible participants take part at no cost.

**Phase I — Targeted Care (Days 1–30)**
The first 30 days establish a baseline and start the work: a full evaluation, hands-on treatment, strength and rehab training, and a nutritional consultation.

**Phase II — Whole Body Care (Days 31–60)**
Days 31–60 build on that foundation with a mid-point evaluation and a fuller range of recovery — strength and rehab, cold plunge, gym access, F.I.T. therapy, and continued nutritional support.

**Phase III — Maintenance Care (Days 61–90 + beyond)**
Days 61–90 lock in the gains with a final evaluation, ongoing treatment and training, infrared sauna and recovery services, and a long-term care plan. Beyond Day 90, we continue maintenance care for as long as we can.
